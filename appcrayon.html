AppCrayon Stylus for Kids

The AppCrayon was an ergonomically-designed stylus for kids to help them learn to hold their pencil or pen correctly. Popular with schools and occupational therapists, the AppCrayon was sold in over 14,000 stores and 3,000 schools worldwide.

  • Worked with product designer, teachers, occupational therapists, and kids on the design.
  • Marketed the AppCrayon via online, print, and other media
  • Given an Editor's Choice Award and a 4-star rating by PC Mag, and was featured as part of their favorites from New York Toy Fair 2012
