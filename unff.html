United Nations
Forum on Forests 3

Working Group Paper

As Coordinator for the Sustainable Forestry Initiative Program through the American Forest & Paper Association (now independent), I had the opportunity to work with a variety of stakeholders from private industry, nonprofits, and government. One special project that I worked on was providing compiling, writing, editing, and advice for the working group papers preparing for the United Nations Forum on Forests 3.

  • This work was done under the supervision of Marvin Brown, former State Forester for Missouri, and future Forester for the state of Oregon.
  • These papers were compiled in conjunction with the United Nations Forum on Forests department.
  • Some of the papers I assisted on can be found here.
